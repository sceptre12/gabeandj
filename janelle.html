About Janelle

Janelle is a native born Jamaican, raised in Ft. Lauderdale, FL. Born to Everton and Yvonne Cunningham; she is the only girl and the youngest of 3. Her brothers are Ricky and Reynaldo. From a young age, Janelle's parents realized that she was a natural performer. During her school days she did many activities such as dance, cheerleading, color guard and band. To this day, you can always catch Janelle dancing and (pretending) to sing to whatever music is around her. There's not a song she doesn't know. Janelle has graduated from Florida International University with a BS in Biomedical Engineering. She is currently a Clinical Analyst at Mount Sinai Medical Center in Miami Beach.
