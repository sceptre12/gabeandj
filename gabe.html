About Gabriel

Gabriel is son to Jean & Esther Augustin; and brother to Renaud, Gilbert, Jennifer, Esther, Allens, and Bernute Augustin. He was born and raised in Miami, FL. He graduated from Florida International University with a BS in Civil Engineering & MS in Construction Management. Gabriel is a a Project Engineer at Suffolk Construction Company Inc. Gabriel is fascinated with music of all flavors and origins, lives to play percussion instruments, and practices Brazillian Jiu-Jitsu more then he sleeps.
